Your Journey to Becoming a Developer Starts Here

At The Coding Dojo, we offer immersive coding bootcamps designed to transform beginners into professional developers. Join us and take the first step towards your new career!

Explore Our Courses

Comprehensive Curriculum

Our curriculum is carefully structured to take students from the fundamentals through to real-world application. Each module focuses on practical skills — from programming basics and algorithms to web development, databases and deployment. Students complete hands-on projects every week, build a portfolio, and receive regular feedback so they graduate with both the knowledge and the artifacts employers care about.

Experienced Instructors

All of our instructors are industry practitioners who bring real-world experience into the classroom. They provide live demonstrations, mentor students one-on-one, run code reviews and lead pair-programming sessions. Instructors also share hiring insights, help with interview preparation and offer ongoing career guidance to support each student’s growth.

Comfortable Study Space

We provide a calm, well-equipped learning environment with modern workstations, reliable internet and plenty of collaborative space. Students can access quiet zones for focused study and communal areas for group work. We also prioritise accessibility, comfortable seating and scheduled breaks so learners can maintain concentration and wellbeing throughout intensive classes.

Employment References

Beyond teaching technical skills, we help students transition to work by providing references, CV reviews and connections to our employer network. Graduates receive personalised help with job search strategies, mock interviews and introductions to companies hiring for junior roles — increasing the chances of meaningful placements after course completion.
